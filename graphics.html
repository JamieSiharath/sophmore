Evaluation of web graphics

Site evaluated: Ink+Volt

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on the page are there is appeal and draw in viewers. The visuals are animated to be eye-catching and asthetically pleasing. From the header the viewer is welcomed with the company name, "Ink + Volt" overlayed atop a video. As stated before the website draws in the viewer, and gives off a welcoming vibe. Each image used to display their products are proffesional and well done.

Guidelines for effective use of graphics

  1. Yes- Graphics fit the purpose, organization and style of the page.
  2. No- Large graphics have been avoided.
  3. Yes- Graphics help to guide the viewers' focus
  4. No- Overly bright and potentially "obnoxious" images have been avoided
  5. No- Graphics have not been used for textual content, other than the banner.
  6. Yes- Alternate text is present for all images.
  7. Yes- Where text appears in graphics (the banner) there is ample contrast.
